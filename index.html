üí° Key Insights

Two Ways to Think About Functions:

1. As a Machine: Put in x, get out y. Linear functions add/multiply by fixed amounts. Quadratic functions square the input.

2. As a Pattern: Linear patterns grow steadily. Quadratic patterns accelerate - they grow slowly then quickly (or vice versa).
